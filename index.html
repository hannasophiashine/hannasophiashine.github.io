About Me:

Hello! My name is Hanna-Sophia Shine and I am the lab manager for the Snedeker Lab (since June 23). I graduated from the University of Maryland, College Park, in May 2023 with a B.S. in Psychology and a minor in classical mythology. I (like a lot of people!) am interested in how we link meaning to language. There seem to be a million and one ways to look at this question but I am specifically interested in exploring this (a) from a developmental perspective, (b) within the intersection between syntax & semantics, & (c) with a focus on events and event structure. The world we experience is filled with events! We are constantly participating in or observing events in some form or another. I am interested in how young children extract information from these events and how this interacts with language.
